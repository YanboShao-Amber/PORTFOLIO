SELECTED WORKS

  • MANAGING UP

    · GAME TYPE : IDLE GAME · DEMO PLATFORM : itch.io

    A satirical idle game where you exploit coworkers for KPI to get promoted, featuring a stealth "Work-Safe" mode disguised as a notepad to slack off and vent office stress on the clock.

  • RAINFALL ECHOES

    · Game Type: A Third Person Horror Deception Game · Game Platform: PC

    In the far future, raindrops mysteriously fell on the arid city. Since then, the humans began to wander around the open city, and there was no sign of stopping. Players will play the role of a detective and investigate the truth of the event.

  • VERMILLION SOMA

    · Game Art / Concept Art

    SOMA is a three-player cooperative alien planet survival exploration game, in which players can choose from three different professions and enter game dungeons in groups of three. On the map, the three players are, and they need to collaborate and share resources to terminate the dungeon boss and complete the game.

  • BLACK SEA

    · Concept Design

    An unknown territory has always hidden an unexplored forbidden zone, known as the "Black Sea". This "Black Sea" was said like a shadow at the corner of the area. People entering here are like hiding in darkness, merging into darkness, and there seems to be some mysterious power in the darkness that pursues the people here.

  • DICE OR DEATH

    · Platform: PC  ·  Team: Endless Ladder Studio · Awards: Best Art & Best Popularity — Mini Game Competition

    A strategy roguelike game on PC. The player is like a small dice — they need to roll the dice so that the numbers on different sides can come into contact with different nodes on the map, aiming to obtain the highest score possible.
